# IT Support Field Labs

A practical IT support and small business infrastructure lab portfolio focused on networking, printers, POS systems, CCTV/IP cameras, VLAN segmentation, DHCP, ACLs, Windows setup, backups, and troubleshooting.

## Purpose

This repository documents hands-on labs designed to simulate real-world IT support and small business infrastructure scenarios.

The goal is to practice technical support, infrastructure planning, configuration, testing, troubleshooting, and documentation.

## Scenario Structure

This repository is organized as a connected small-business IT support case study.

Lab 01 presents the full network and infrastructure design. Labs 02, 03, and 04 zoom into specific support areas inside that environment: network printers, POS systems, and CCTV/IP cameras.

The goal is to show both high-level infrastructure planning and practical hands-on support workflows.

## Labs

| Lab | Title | Role in the Scenario |
|---|---|---|
| Lab 01 | Small Business Network Design | Full infrastructure overview: VLANs, DHCP, inter-VLAN routing, ACLs, servers, printers, POS, CCTV, and Wi-Fi |
| Lab 02 | Network Printer Configuration | Focused support module for printer setup, IP planning, drivers, and troubleshooting |
| Lab 03 | POS Support Lab | Focused support module for POS workstation, receipt printer, POS server/application access, and peripheral troubleshooting |
| Lab 04 | CCTV / IP Camera Support | Focused support module for IP cameras, NVR/server concept, static IPs, connectivity testing, and CCTV troubleshooting |
